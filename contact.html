Direction from DIA to CLEAR:


These direction info below come from MapQuest.com or from Google.com

  1. Start out going North on PENA BLVD toward TERMINAL W. (0.2 miles)
  2. Stay straight to go onto TERMINAL E. (1.0 miles)
  3. TERMINAL E becomes TERMINAL EAST DEPARTURES. (0.6 miles)
  4. Turn SLIGHT RIGHT onto AIRPORT EXIT. (0.4 miles)
  5. Take PENA BLVD. (3.4 miles)
  6. Take the E-470 N exit- exit number 6B- toward 120TH AVENUE. (0.8 miles)
  7. Merge onto E 470 N (Portions toll). (18.0 miles)
  8. E 470 N becomes NORTHWEST PKWY W (Portions toll). (8.5 miles)
  9. NORTHWEST PKWY W becomes STORAGE TEK DR/S 96TH ST. (0.3 miles)
  10. Merge onto US-36 W. (6.2 miles)
  11. Merge onto FOOTHILLS PKWY/CO-157 N toward C U STADIUM. (2.4 miles)
  12. Turn LEFT onto ARAPAHOE AVE/CO-7 N. (0.3 miles)
  13. Turn RIGHT onto 38TH ST. (less than 0.1 miles)
  14. Turn LEFT onto EXPOSITION DR. (0.2 miles)
  15. End at the Center for Innovation and Creativity, a large 2 story building. the main entrance is located on the southwest side of the building.

Direction from Denver to CLEAR:

  1. Take US-36 W to Boulder and take exit onto Foothills Parkway just before entering Boulder.
  2. Drive north on Foothills Parkway.
  3. Pass Baseline and Colorado Bvld, at traffic light turn left onto Arapahoe.
  4. Drive west on Arapahoe, immediately at first traffic intersection, turn right onto 38th street
  5. Turn left onto Exposition Dr.
  6. The Center for Innovation and Creativity is on your left side, a large 2 story building. the main entrance is located on the southwest side of the building.
