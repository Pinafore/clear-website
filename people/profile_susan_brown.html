RESEARCH INTERESTS: Computational linguistics and natural language processing, in particular semantic issues such as word sense disambiguation, semantic representations, and ontology development. I am also interested in the use of psycholinguistic and corpus linguistic methodologies and insights to improve resources for statistical machine learning.
